Welcome to SustainableMiles.

The place where you earn vehicles by completing tasks.

I see that you have an account with us, so why not get started with us now? Click on the buttons to navigate your way, or you can use the navigaton bar on top.

Who are we?

We are a student project that is trying to make the world a better place by reducing the amount of carbon emissions that are being released into the atmosphere. We do this by giving you the opportunity to earn vehicles by completing tasks. The more tasks you complete, the more vehicles you earn. The more vehicles you earn, the more carbon emissions you reduce.

Why are we doing this?

The Earth is facing a critical environmental crisis largely due to the increasing levels of carbon emissions in the atmosphere. Carbon emissions primarily come from human activities such as burning fossil fuels for energy, deforestation, industrial processes, and various other anthropogenic sources. The consequences of elevated carbon emissions are contributing to global climate change, which poses severe threats to ecosystems, weather patterns, and overall environmental stability.

We are doing this because we want to make the world a better place. We want to reduce the amount of carbon emissions that are being released into the atmosphere, and we want to do this by giving you the opportunity to earn vehicles by completing tasks.

How do we work?

We work by giving you the opportunity to earn vehicles by completing tasks. When you complete a task, you can add your completed task to your task progress, which is where the points are earned. Those points can be used to purchase vehicles. The more points you earn, the more vehicles you earn.

Since you are logged in with us, you have the opportunity to add tasks and create vehicles for other users to earn.
